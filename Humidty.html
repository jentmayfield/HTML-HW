Humidity

There is not as much direct correlation between humidity and latitude. There appears to be some correlations between the wind and humidity. Higher winds decrease humidity. Cities north of the equator experience higher wind speeds than those south of the equator. More cities north of the equator experience more humidity as compared to cities south of the equator.
